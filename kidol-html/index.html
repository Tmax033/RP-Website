Kidland

An Augmented Reality-Based Approach for Smart Ordering for Toy Store.

KidLand, identifying the requirements of the improvements of exsisting eCommerce solutions and to fill the gaps, KidLand was developed. The application is powered by the help of the Augmented reality and Machine-learning and Natural language processing. The ordinary online shopping experience will be completely change with the advanced AR features!!!
